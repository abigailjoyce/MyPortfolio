AVIS FALLS
Tanap Avis Falls, located in Bangui, Ilocos Norte, is a picturesque natural attraction that captivates visitors with its serene beauty. Tucked away in a remote area, the falls are surrounded by lush forests and rugged terrain, creating a peaceful and secluded atmosphere. The cascading waters of Tanap Avis Falls offer a refreshing sight and a tranquil retreat, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The falls' stunning surroundings, combined with the cool, clear waters, provide an ideal setting for relaxation and exploration.
