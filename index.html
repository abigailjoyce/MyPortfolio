About Me

Hello! I'm Abigail Joyce Palting, a curious soul with a passion for learning and exploring life's wonders. I find joy in riding a motorcycle, and I absolutely love the thrill of the open road and the sense of freedom it brings. As an adventurer at heart, I enjoy exploring new shores and discovering hidden gems in different places. Whether it's cruising along scenic coastal routes or venturing into the mountains, I'm always up for an adventure. In addition to my love for motorcycling and exploration, I have a deep passion for photography and cinematography. Capturing moments and telling stories through visuals is something that brings me immense joy and fulfillment.
